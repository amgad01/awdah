Awdah Favicon Pack
==================

Files included:
  awdah-favicon-forest-gold.svg   — Dark green + gold (recommended)
  awdah-favicon-midnight-gold.svg — Deep navy/black + gold
  awdah-favicon-navy-sand.svg     — Navy blue + sand gold
  awdah-favicon-oud-amber.svg     — Dark brown + amber

Usage in index.html:
  <link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="/favicon.svg" />

SVG favicons scale perfectly to all resolutions (16px to 512px).
Rename your chosen file to favicon.svg and place it in your public/ folder.
